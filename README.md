# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 🔎 Project Overview

This project analyzes ~9,800 sales transactions from a retail e-commerce dataset to evaluate revenue performance, customer behavior, and regional trends.

The goal was to build an executive-level dashboard that transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights using Google Sheets.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze revenue trends over time  
- Identify top-performing regions and product categories  
- Evaluate customer segmentation and repeat behavior  
- Measure customer lifetime value (LTV)  
- Present KPI-driven insights in a dashboard format  

---

## 📁 Dataset

**Source:** Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)  
**Records:** ~9,800  

### Key Fields Used:
- Order Date  
- Customer ID  
- Region  
- Segment  
- Category  
- Sales  

---

## 🛠 Data Preparation & Feature Engineering

Performed structured data transformation in Google Sheets:

- Created Order Month, Order Year, and Order Quarter  
- Standardized categorical fields (TRIM + UPPER)  
- Calculated Customer Lifetime Revenue  
- Created Repeat Customer Flag  
- Identified High Value Customers  
- Built Month Sort Key for proper chronological sorting  
- Aggregated metrics using QUERY and Pivot Tables  

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue  
- Total Orders  
- Unique Customers  
- Average Order Value (AOV)  
- Repeat Customer Rate  
- High Value Customer %  
- Top Revenue Region  
- Top Revenue Category  
- Revenue by Year  
- Month-over-Month Growth  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

The final dashboard includes:

- Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)  
- Revenue by Category (Bar Chart)  
- Revenue by Region (Column Chart)  
- Revenue by Segment (Pie Chart)  
- Top 10 Customers by Revenue  
- Executive KPI Summary Section  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The West region generates the highest revenue contribution.  
- Technology is the leading revenue-driving category.  
- Consumer segment contributes over 50% of total revenue.  
- Revenue shows a consistent upward growth trend from 2015–2018.  
- Top 10 customers contribute a significant portion of overall revenue.
